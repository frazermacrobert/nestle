About This Experience

This interactive simulation demonstrates several L&D gamification principles:

  • Time pressure to simulate real-world constraints
  • Visual feedback (pulsing edges, trait discovery) for immediate reinforcement
  • Branching narratives to create multiple discovery paths
  • Reflective assessment (Round Two) to evaluate decision-making
  • Hidden scoring to reduce performance anxiety and encourage exploration

These same principles can transform compliance training, onboarding programs, and leadership development.
